Ongoing - 2026 Featured Project

Library Digitization Initiative

We are currently in the process of digitizing our rarest Tamil manuscripts and historical gazettes from the early 20th century. This project ensures that our 112-year-old collection remains safe for future generations and accessible to researchers worldwide.

January 15, 2026

Pongal Cultural Literature Meet

Join us for an evening of classical Tamil poetry reading and a discussion on the history of Sanmarga Sangam's foundation in the Thiruvannamalai region.

Community Reading Circle

The library hosts a monthly gathering for students and local readers to share insights on contemporary Tamil prose and classic literature.
