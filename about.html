About the Trust

Our Founder

Founded in 1914 by Pu. Pa. Masilamani Mudaliar, the trust was established to bring the light of Tamil literature to the people of Thiruvannamalai.

Over the last century, we have transitioned from a local reading room to one of the most significant archival libraries in the region.
