About the Trust

Preserving Tamil Literary Heritage for over 112 years

Our History

The **Samarasa Sanmarga Sangam** was established in **1914** by the visionary **Pu. Pa. Masilamani Mudaliar**. In an era where access to formal knowledge was limited, Mudaliar recognized the profound need for a community-centered library that could house the intellectual wealth of Tamil literature.

As the first library to serve both the **Thiruvannamalai** and **Vellore** districts, the institution became a foundational pillar for local scholars, students, and historians. For over a century, the library has operated as a non-profit charitable trust, ensuring that the light of knowledge is available to everyone without discrimination.

"Our mission is to safeguard the rare manuscripts and literary masterpieces of Tamil Nadu, ensuring they remain accessible for the next generation of seekers."
