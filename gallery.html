Heritage Gallery

Exploring over a century of literature, service, and community.

Founded in November 1914, the Sangam has preserved the intellectual wealth of Tamil literature for generations. From welcoming Pandit Jawaharlal Nehru in 1936 to the recent digitization of 172 rare books, this gallery documents our enduring journey.
