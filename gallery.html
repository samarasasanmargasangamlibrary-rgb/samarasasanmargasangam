Glimpses of our 100-Year Journey

In 1914, the Samarasa Sanmarga Sangam was established as a collective effort by the village community, initiated by the visionary Pu. Pa. Masilamani Mudaliar. They came together to build a community library to preserve the intellectual wealth of Tamil literature.
