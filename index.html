The First Library of Thiruvannamalai & Vellore Districts

Our Legacy Since 1914

Established in 1914 by Pu. Pa. Masilamani Mudaliar, we are a cornerstone of Tamil Nadu’s literary history. As the first library founded in the historic Thiruvannamalai and Vellore districts, we have stood for over 112 years as a guardian of knowledge.

Home to an unbelievable collection of rare Tamil literature, we remain dedicated to the pursuit of learning and the service of our community.
