The First Library of Thiruvannamalai & Vellore Districts

Our Legacy Since 1914

Established in 1914 by Pu. Pa. Masilamani Mudaliar, we are a cornerstone of Tamil Nadu’s literary history. Home to an unbelievable collection of rare Tamil literature, we have been dedicated to the pursuit of knowledge and the service of the community for over a century.
