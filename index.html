The Gateway to Tamil Heritage

Welcome to Samarasa Sanmarga Sangam, one of the oldest and most historically significant libraries in Tamil Nadu. Founded by the noble Pu. Pa. Masilamani Mudaliar, our institution was the pioneer of the library movement in the Thiruvannamalai and Vellore districts.

For over a century, we have remained a steadfast guardian of Tamil culture, housing an unbelievable collection of rare literature and historical archives. Today, we continue our mission as a charitable trust, serving students, researchers, and book lovers with the same passion that started in 1914.
