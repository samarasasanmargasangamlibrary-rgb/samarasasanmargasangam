Our Rare Collection

Preserving over 150 years of Tamil intellectual history.

Featured Historical Works

In 2015, the International Institute of Tamil Studies identified and digitized 172 extremely rare books from our library, ensuring their survival for future generations.

Note: Our full archive contains over 3,000 volumes. The works listed above represent the oldest and most rare manuscripts recently restored by the government's preservation project.

Samarasa Sanmarga Sangam

A premier charitable trust and historical library dedicated to the preservation of Tamil literature since 1914.

Established on November 27, 1914, through the collective efforts of the Pudupalayam village community.
